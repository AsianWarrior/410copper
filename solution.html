The Solution

PercussAtSight will offer an infinite well of practice material,
it eliminates the need for expensive and various books, while generating
entirely random percussion scores and drum charts to keep musicians
engaged, challenged, and prepared. For instructors, it enables precise,
customized teaching, enabling them to teach exactly how they want to.
With the ability to be fully customizable, PercussAtSight accommodates
individual goals and learning preferences, making percussion practice dynamic
and effective for percussionists of all levels.
